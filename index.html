How to play?

Pick which pool (from the above buttons) you want to play

Click “PlayOnline”

Enter your Nickname you want on your sheet

Enter an email. PLEASE MAKE SURE IT’S A GOOD EMAIL, OR YOU WILL NOT GET A COPY OF YOUR PICKS

Enter your Telephone Number (This way we can contact you)

Pick a Tiebreaker for the last game on the Sheet.

Example: You think the Away team will score 27 – the Home Team will score 20. You would put 47

Click on "Submit"

Click on "Play Another Sheet"

Play as many sheets as you want

Click on "Donate" & pay the total amount once you’re done. DO NOT SEND SEPARATE PAYMENTS. If you play all 3 pools sends 1 payment for all picks.

You play 5 Pro & 1 College & 2 Basketball send donation for 8 picks

Once done with all your picks click “Donate Here” button and donate the number of sheets you played.
